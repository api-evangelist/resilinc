Belden transformed its supply chain decision-making by connecting spend, revenue exposure, and supplier risk data into a unified model. Rather than managing isolated events, the company now uses composite scoring to dynamically classify suppliers and understand business impact, reducing response time from days to minutes. This shift enables earlier action on disruptions and demonstrates how data-driven frameworks improve organizational decision-making across procurement operations.
